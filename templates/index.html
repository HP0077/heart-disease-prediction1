Heart Disease - A Growing Concern

Heart disease is the leading cause of death worldwide, with over 18 million deaths annually. Factors such as unhealthy lifestyles, poor diet, lack of exercise, and genetic predisposition contribute to the rising numbers.

According to the World Health Organization, cardiovascular diseases account for nearly 31% of all global deaths. The need for early diagnosis and prevention has never been more urgent.

SafeHeartAI is designed to help bridge this gap by leveraging AI to detect early signs of heart disease, giving users actionable insights for a healthier future.
